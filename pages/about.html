ABOUT QUACK FOR HELP

Quack for Help was founded to help students in CS 146 through times of joy, despair, and a full range of other emotions. Whether you are crying because of the upcoming quiz, happy because of your score on the previous quiz, or any emotion in between, we have a quiz for you!

Patients struggling with mental health often have a more difficult time managing their overall health. In order to manage yourself better, take a quiz!
